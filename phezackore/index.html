Phezackore Hunt

  • High Concept: Gather all of of the phezackores with limited resources!
  • Themes: Invisibility, guessing game gone wrong.
  • Mandated Variety: This is a luck-based puzzle game which uses the mouse and clickable buttons for input. There is a lot of randomness in the gameplay, and it has collector/achiever play styles.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun testing your luck, memory, and planning skills!
  • Gameplay:
  • You begin with 150 resource points and 100 health points; if either reach 0, you lose. If you manage to gather all 20 phezackores, you win and 100 bonus points are added to your score. When the game ends, your remaining RP and HP are added to your score.
  • Every search consumes (2 + # of tiles in search) resources.
  • To search, select an area of tiles by clicking. Use the range slider to adjust the size of the area to examine, then pick a confidence level and click the search button.
  • If no phezackore are in the searched area and you guessed with any amount of confidence, they will temporarily appear and attack you! On the other hand, if you guessed correctly and with any amount of confidence, you are rewarded:
  • Very Confident-> Penalty: -8*(range+1) HP
    •          Reward: +20*(# found) score, +8 RP
  • Confident-> Penalty: -4*(range+1) HP
    •        Reward: +10*(# found) score, +4 RP
  • Slightly Confident-> Penalty: -2*(range+1) HP
    •            Reward: +5*(# found) score, +2 RP
  • If you search in a broad area (range>1), the phezackores are likely to move around, so you can't depend on never checking the same place twice!
  • Prototype Goal: Test how a player's confidence in their guesses adds interest to a guessing game.
  • Features: The options of range and confidence, and the inclusion of both a resource and health bar (uncommon in guessing games).
  • Setting/Premise: You are a researcher who has come across an incredible new species: the phezackore! You have confirmed that they're currently living in a moonlit expanse in the middle of a dense forest. These creatures have special bodies which allow them to turn completely invisible, and they appear to be dangerous. Most refused to believe your claims about the phezackores, so you only got so much funding to gather them for research. Be careful; phezackores seem to be very protective of their kind, so they won't let you get away once you start gathering. It's all or nothing...good luck!
  • Target Audience: Those who enjoy hard games which require luck and good memory.
  • Play Time: About 5-15 minutes.
  • Strategies:
  • Search with slight confidence if you just want a hint as to where some are, and take note of where the phezackores appear!
  • Running dangerously low on resources? Increase your confidence, lower your range of search, and hope you're lucky.
  • Use the flower/rock locations to help you remember where you've searched already, and search with low range (0-1) so the phezackores don't move around!
