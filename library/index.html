Spellcaster's Quest

  • High Concept: Use spells to seal away evil and avoid getting caught.
  • Theme: Bookworm.
  • Player Experience Goals: To fearfully face enemies with the power of spellcasting and seal them away with smart use of it.
  • Gameplay:
    • Move with the arrow keys and use spells to avoid the enemy! To cast spells, hold either W, A, S, or D. Without letting go, enter the 4-key spell code. Spells include making bookshelves translucent (hold S, enter adad), a shrinking spell (hold S, enter awdd), a warping spell (hold W, enter ssaa), and a enemy sight spell (hold D, enter sasa). Each spell lasts 10 seconds, and only one spell may be in effect at a time. Cancel the current spell by pressing X. To win, you must lead all enemies into the red magic circle.
    • In the next version, there will be a proper mana/sprit energy system and each enemy will be different, requiring a unique method to seal each away (relating to the book the character came from).

  • Prototype Goal: Test a semi-realistic spellcasting system.
  • Features: The ability to use a variety of spells.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a young spellcaster who has entered an ancient library in search of an ancient spell! However, upon opening one book, you've unleashed an evil demon which has brought to life evil characters from the books in the library! You must now seal them away.
  • Target Audience: Fans of RPGs, stealth games, or magic.
  • Play Time: About 2 minutes
  • Tips/Strategies:
    • After an enemy sees you, try warping to the other side of a bookshelf to escape!
    • In a pinch? Make yourself small and hide behind the bookshelves along the top wall!
