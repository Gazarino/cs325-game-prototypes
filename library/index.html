Spellcaster's Quest

  • High Concept: Use spells to seal away evil and avoid getting caught.
  • Theme: Bookworm.
  • Player Experience Goals: To fearfully face enemies with the power of spellcasting and seal them away with smart use of it.
  • Gameplay:
    • Use spells to avoid the enemy! To do this, hold either W, A, S, or D. Without letting go, enter the 4-key spell code. Spells include making bookshelves translucent (hold S, enter adad), a shrinking spell (hold S, enter awdd), a warping spell (hold W, enter ssaa), and a enemy sight spell (hold D, enter sasa). Each spell lasts 10 seconds.
